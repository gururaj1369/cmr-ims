Campus & Classrooms

Located in the Heart of the city. Fully furnished college and hostel Qualified staff, according to INC norms. All the class rooms equipped with modern teaching aids spacious auditorium for curricular and co-curricular activities. Good clinical facility with parent general hospital. Well equipped computer laboratory.

Sports And Cultural Events

Sports and extracurricular activities are encouraged to all students to participate in programs at the university in both state and national level. Outdoor and indoor recreational and cultural programs are organised regularly according to the academic schedule of the respective colleges.

Hostel Facilities

There is a separate hostel in the college premises and it is compulsory for all the students to stay in the hostel. 100 Students occupancy dining hall. The hostel provides a decent & hygienic atmosphere for the students, water facility is available round the clock. Wholesome and balanced nutrition is provided in a hygienic environment.

Placement

Placement of students after graduation in this rewarding career is another on-going exercise that the NSS Nursing undertakes through constant interaction with the employing agencies. International student's orientation and reception programmes.

Library And Reading Room

A Good Library is available for students to use all the latest books of B.Sc. Nursing, and all other topics in Medical Surgical Nursing, Pediatric Nursing, Community Health Nursing and Mental Health Nursing and more books provided to them.

Transportation

Own school & collage bus facility is available for transporting the students form collage to hospitals, hostel and for other extracurricular activities.
